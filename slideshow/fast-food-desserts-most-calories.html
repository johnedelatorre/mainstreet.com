The Fast Food Desserts With the Most Calories

  • Matt Brownell
  • Jun 6, 2011 7:00 AM EDT

  • Counting Calories

    In New York and a few other locales, counting calories is easy. Every large chain restaurant is required to list calorie and fat content on its menu for each item. And while similar menu rules are on the way for the rest of the country as a result of the health care reform law, for now most of America isn’t being presented with nutrition information when they go out to eat. And that’s especially troublesome when it comes to dessert. Sure, everyone knows that ice cream and mammoth chocolate shakes aren’t good for you. But we’d imagine many people would opt for a fruit cup if they saw the nutrition info associated with the dessert offerings of some fast food restaurants. We’d recommend checking out the nutrition info on these chain’s websites to see how your favorites stack up. But for now, to show you just how bad it can be, we’ve looked at the nation’s biggest fast food chains to find the top 11 desserts with the most calories. Please note that when there are several versions of a line of desserts offered by a given chain (for instance, Dairy Queen's line of Blizzards), we’ve only listed the highest-calorie offering. Photo Credit: Linda Avriani
    11th Worst: Whataburger Chocolate Malt (Large)

  • 11th Worst: Whataburger Chocolate Malt (Large)

    Calories: 1,050 Fat: 25 g Saturated Fat: 18 g Sugar: 175 g Full Nutrition Facts Yes, we’re at the bottom of our list and we’re already over the thousand mark. This is one of three desserts on the list with 1,050 calories, making for a three-way tie at eleventh. Photo Credit: Whataburger.com
    11th Worst: Krispy Kreme Chocolate Chocolate Chiller (20 oz.)

  • 11th Worst: Krispy Kreme Chocolate Chocolate Chiller (20 oz.)

    Calories: 1,050 Fat: 42 g Saturated Fat: 36 g Sugar: 100 g Full Nutrition Facts “Hey, does this have chocolate in it?” Photo Credit: krispykreme.com
    11th Worst: Krispy Kreme Arctic Avalanche Chocolate Chip Cookie Dough Kool Kreme (20 oz.)

  • 11th Worst: Krispy Kreme Arctic Avalanche Chocolate Chip Cookie Dough Kool Kreme (20 oz.)

    Calories: 1,050 Fat: 38 g Saturated Fat: 17 g Sugar: 116 g Full Nutrition Facts Maybe if actual avalanches had chocolate chip cookie dough, people wouldn’t mind getting caught in them. (Shown here is the Oreo version, which has a mere 860 calories.) Photo Credit: krispykreme.com
    8th Worst: Cold Stone Creamery Oreo Crème Ice Cream (Gotta-Have-It)

  • 8th Worst: Cold Stone Creamery Oreo Crème Ice Cream (Gotta-Have-It)

    Calories: 1,060 Fat: 74 g Saturated Fat: 35 g Sugar: 91 g Full Nutrition Facts “Gotta-Have-It” is actually the name of the largest size at Cold Stone. You don’t actually gotta have it, though, unless your goal is to gain weight as quickly as possible. Then we suppose you do actually gotta have it. Photo Credit: mattgarber
    7th Worst:  McDonald’s Chocolate Thick Shake (32 oz.)

  • 7th Worst:  McDonald’s Chocolate Thick Shake (32 oz.)

    Calories: 1,160 Fat: 27 g Saturated Fat: 16 g Sugar: 168 g Full Nutrition Facts You didn’t think we could have an article about unhealthy food and not have McDonald’s show up, did you? Photo Credit: mcdonalds.com
    6th Worst:  Baskin Robbins Oreo Layered Sundae (347 grams)

  • 6th Worst:  Baskin Robbins Oreo Layered Sundae (347 grams)

    Calories: 1,330 Fat: 61 g Saturated Fat: 31 g Sugar: 146 g Full Nutrition Facts This isn’t the last we’ll see of Baskin Robbins on this list. They’re good at what they do. Photo Credit: baskinrobbins.com
    5th Worst: A&W Reese’s Peanut Butter Fudge Blendrrr (32 oz.)

  • 5th Worst: A&W Reese’s Peanut Butter Fudge Blendrrr (32 oz.)

    Calories: 1,360 Fat: 79 g Saturated Fat: 57 g Sugar: 131 g Full Nutrition Facts The extra “rrr” at the end is supposed to indicate that it will make you cold while you’re drinking it. But if you drink enough of them, you’ll build up some insulation pretty quick. Photo Credit: Hugh Lee
    4th Worst: Dairy Queen Turtle Pecan Cluster Blizzard (Large)

  • 4th Worst: Dairy Queen Turtle Pecan Cluster Blizzard (Large)

    Calories: 1,460 Fat: 81 g Saturated Fat: 42 g Sugar: 122 g Full Nutrition Facts This was the unhealthiest of DQ’s lineup of Blizzards, which also includes the Georgia Mud Fudge (1,450 calories) the French Silk Pie (1,320). I don’t know why you would make fudge out of mud or a pie out of silk, but I don’t have a good grasp on French or Georgian cuisine. Photo Credit: dairyqueen.com
    3rd Worst:  Sonic Double Stuf Oreo Sonic Blast (Large)

  • 3rd Worst:  Sonic Double Stuf Oreo Sonic Blast (Large)

    Calories: 1,610 Fat: 93 g Saturated Fat: 49 g Sugar: 148 g Full Nutrition Facts I remember how excited I was when Double Stuf Oreos started being sold. It never occurred to me to put them in a blender and turn them into a drink, but fortunately Sonic came through for America. Photo Credit: sonicdrivein.com
    2nd Worst:  Baskin Robbins Fudge Brownie 31 Below (Large)

  • 2nd Worst:  Baskin Robbins Fudge Brownie 31 Below (Large)

    Calories: 1,900 Fat: 80 g Saturated Fat: 39 Sugar: 225 g Full Nutrition Facts If you're on a 2,000-calorie diet and you have one of these, you can pretty much have a few saltines and then you’ll have to call it a day. And yes, there’s a dessert on this list that has more than 1,900 calories. Photo Credit: baskinrobbins.com
    The Worst Dessert: Cold Stone Creamery PB&C (Gotta-Have-It)

  • The Worst Dessert: Cold Stone Creamery PB&C (Gotta-Have-It)

    Calories: 2,010 Fat: 131 g Saturated Fat: 68 g Sugar: 153 g Full Nutrition Facts There are almost as many calories in this as there are years since the birth of Jesus. Just think about that. Photo Credit: coldstonecreamery.com
