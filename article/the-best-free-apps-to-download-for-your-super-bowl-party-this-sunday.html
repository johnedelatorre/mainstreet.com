The Best Free Apps to Download for Your Super Bowl Party This Sunday

  • Kristin Colella
  • Jan 30, 2015 9:47 AM EST
ADVERTISEMENT

NEW YORK (MainStreet) — Are you planning on hosting a Super Bowl bash this Sunday as the New England Patriots battle the Seattle Seahawks? If you've been procrastinating on party prep, it's time to get started on a few important details, including creating a guest list, purchasing alcohol and figuring out which tasty dishes to whip up. Luckily, you can download some handy smartphone apps to make the party-planning process a whole lot easier. Here are a few of our favorites to check out as game day nears.

Grocery iQ

To make those trips to the grocery store in the coming days a bit easier, consider downloading the Grocery iQ app by Coupons.com, which is available on Android and iOS devices. The app allows you to create digital grocery lists easily so that you don't forget any essential ingredients. Then when you're shopping, you can check off items on your list to keep track of what you've placed in your cart. When you tap "Checkout," the items that you checked off will be cleared from your list, and the items you didn't buy will remain on your list so you know what still needs to be purchased next time.

You can also use the app to locate nearby grocery stores, scan the bar codes of items you'd like to add to your list (such as something already in your pantry or fridge that you're running low on) and search for coupons.

RetailMeNot

The RetailMeNot app can help you keep costs low as you shop for food and party supplies. Available on Android and iOS devices, it offers coupons and deals from more than 50,000 retailers. To find out what deals are currently available, you can browse stores by category or simply type in the name of your favorite retailer in the search box. The app also has a map feature so that you can see the deals closest to your location.

Cocktail Flow

If you really want to impress your guests this Sunday, how about concocting some refreshing game-day libations? The app Cocktail Flow, available on Android and iOS devices, offers drink recipes with step-by-step instructions that even novice mixologists can handle. You can search for your favorite cocktails or browse suggested ones by various categories such as color, base drink (e.g., brandy, champagne or tequila) and type (e.g., classical, creamy, shooter, tropical). To save money and prevent any unnecessary trips to the liquor store, Cocktail Flow even allows you to check off the cocktail ingredients that you already have at home to find out what drinks you can create.

Although the basic features of the app are free, for an extra $0.99 you can purchase "The Big Game," a premium package that provides recipes for football-themed cocktails perfect for Super Bowl parties.

Food Network In the Kitchen

The yummy snacks and appetizers served at a Super Bowl party are almost as important as the game itself. If you need some creative suggestions for what to put out on the table, the Food Network In the Kitchen app can help—even if you're an inexperienced cook. You'll find thousands of recipes from Food Network chefs and shows, including some with video instruction. You can search by ingredient or by chef (such as Alton Brown, Rachael Ray and Bobby Flay), or browse by category such as appetizers, chicken and desserts.

The app is also currently offering special game-day recipes under fun categories such as "Kickoff Snacks," "Guilt-Free Game Grub," "Championship Chili Bowls" and "Cheer-Worthy Desserts."

Food Network In the Kitchen is available on iOS and Android devices.

DrinkFly

If your game-day prep has made you too busy to run to the liquor store, you can arrange for your booze to be conveniently delivered to your home in 60 minutes or less with DrinkFly. Available on Android and iOS devices, the app allows you to type in your address to find out which liquor businesses are available for delivery or pick-up in your area. You can place items in your cart and pay for your order directly through the app—simple as that. To make your life even easier, DrinkFly also features a credit card scanner so that you can quickly enter your credit card information.

Evite

If you haven't done so already, it's time to get the word out about your big Super Bowl bash. The Evite app makes it easy to send your guests free digital invites through your smartphone. You can choose from artistic Super Bowl-themed templates and also easily manage your guest list so you know who to expect.

The Evite app can be downloaded on Android and iOS devices.
