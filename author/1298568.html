Zillow is an online home and real estate marketplace dedicated to helping homeowners, home buyers, sellers, renters, real estate agents, mortgage professionals, landlords and property managers find and share vital information.

Zillow's database of more than 110 million U.S. homes includes homes for sale, homes for rent and homes not currently on the market. Zillow also provides a suite of tools to estimate home values, rent and other useful information.

The Zillow Mortgage Marketplace allows borrowers to connect with lenders to find loans and get the best mortgage rates.

Zillow Mobile is the most popular real estate mobile platform today.
