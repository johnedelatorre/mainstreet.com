Marguerite Arnold

Journalist

Follow

Marguerite Arnold is a journalist for The Street and Main Street located in Germany.

Previously, she worked for Reuters covering politics and media. She also worked for Morgan Stanley to help build out their global WAN and develop innovative trading software linking institutional investors to a new kind of auction for vested Google options. She has also worked as a sustainability consultant in several states. Arnold published her first book in 2015, Green: The First 12 Months of Modern American Marijuana Reform, about the pace of change in 2014. She is also obtaining her EMBA from the Frankfurt School of Finance and Management.
