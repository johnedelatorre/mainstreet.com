Janet Al-Saad

Reporter

Follow

Janet Al-Saad writes on technology, personal finance, corporate finance, global trade and macroeconomics. She has interviewed CEOs and CFOs of major publicly listed companies. A former Contributing Editor at TheStreet, she has also served as Managing Editor of Mint.com, reporter and co-anchor on nationally syndicated business news program First Business, U.S. News Editor of Reuters BreakingViews, and Director of News -- Social Media for Univision. She has served as an editor and writer for Franklin Templeton Investments, JPMorgan Chase, American Express, Brown Brothers Harriman and many others.

Janet holds an MBA from the Thunderbird School at Arizona State University.
