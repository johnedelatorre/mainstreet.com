Marilen Cawad

Managing Editor, Partner Relations

Follow

Marilen Cawad manages TheStreet’s relationships with content partners. She creates content for partners and syndicates TheStreet’s articles and videos to relevant partner sites. She also writes personal finance, travel and lifestyle features for TheStreet.

Marilen joined TheStreet in March 2010 as senior editor, after working as news editor at Institutional Investor magazine in New York. She has written and produced reports for Worth magazine, ABC News, CNN World Report and GMA Network.

Marilen was a Knight-Bagehot fellow in economics and business journalism at Columbia University, where she holds a dual degree master's in journalism and international affairs.
