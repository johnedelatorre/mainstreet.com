All in One Credit Card Solution Stratos Card Begins Shipping Today

  • Lauren Lyons Cole
  • Apr 30, 2015 9:00 AM EDT
ADVERTISEMENT

Early adopters of the all in one credit card will soon get to test out the technology. Today, the new Stratos electronic credit card has started shipping to consumers. Smart credit cards allow you to consolidate your entire wallet into one credit card sized device. Stratos cards store an unlimited number of credit cards, debit cards, gift cards and loyalty cards.Other electronic credit cards will ship later this year, including offerings from Coin, Swyp and Plastc. Demand is high, however, and orders are fulfilled on a first come, first serve basis. Given the ease of use and unique security features, it’s no wonder many people are eager to get their hands on one. Fees and features vary between companies, so consumers interested in purchasing an all in one credit card should carefully compare the options before adding their name to a waiting list.
